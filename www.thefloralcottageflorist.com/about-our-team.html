For 25 years, The Floral Cottage has proudly served Ascension Parish and the surrounding areas. We specialize in luxury weddings and events, but also love to share our passion for flowers with the community through our Floral Boutique.

Randee Gagnon

Randee, our Owner and Lead Floral Designer, rediscovered her love for flowers after years as a Speech-Language Pathologist, becoming a second-generation Floral Designer. When she’s not designing, she enjoys exploring the world through food and travel with her husband and two daughters.

The Faces Behind The Emails

Danielle

Danielle is our Lead Event Specialist + Delivery Coordinator. When she’s not here, she loves spending time camping with her family and friends. She’s a mama of 2! Harper is 4, and Eliza was born in August! Danielle is our unofficial “HR” and always keeps us laughing at the shop. You’ll typically find her doing consultations, writing beautiful emails, or mobile ordering chick-fil-a.

Hannah

Hannah is an Event Specialist, Floral Designer, and our Social Media Manager. She loves animals (especially sharks), and all things pink, girly + floral. She’s a cat mom to Mrs. Willow Bean Puff + Moose, and loves spending time with her family and boyfriend. When she’s not here, she’s usually spending time with her Grandma or her cats! When she’s here, she’s typically chatting with brides, designing, or creating content.

Ava

Ava is our Tech + Media Specialist and an Event Specialist. She curates our newsletter, updates our website and chats with brides at inquiry + finalization. She’s always taking notes, solving problems + asking what’s for lunch. She loves freelance writing, singing karaoke and dancing in the street with the King Cake Babies every Mardi Gras!

The Faces Behind The flowers

Becky

Becky is the founder of The Floral Cottage! She first opened the shop in 1999, and is still here doing what she loves, which is playing with flowers! When she’s not here, she loves spending time baking with her grandchildren + doing home projects. We call her Beck-Home-Ecky because she can whip out ANYTHING we possibly need out of nothing. When she’s here, you’ll typically find her whipping out designs in 2 shakes of a lamb’s tail!

Joelle

Joelle has been a designer at The Floral Cottage for longer than she can remember! She loves spending time with her fiancé, Nora, and shopping at the Amazon return center. (then bringing half the stuff she buys to the shop). Her favorite part about her job is expressing her creativity every day! When she’s here, you’ll find her designing, sharing her lunch with everyone, or harvesting chadron from the front yard.

The Floral Cottage has grown to a talented team of event + retail floral designers, wedding/event staff + specialists and shop assistants. You’ll find our team flowering all over Baton Rouge + the surrounding areas.

Our dedication to creating art through flowers could be the perfect addition to your upcoming wedding or event!
